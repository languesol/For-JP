Happy Birthday JP

  • by:Rho
  • date:December 17, 2020
  • 3 Comments

Dear Jean,
on Monday December 7, I woke up thinking about you (as usual) and of what I could do for your birthday that would be different from the usual material stuff. I got the idea to make the creative juices flow and create a professional website for you.

I had two options: use a website builder, like I did for Languesol and pay hefty fees each year or I could try to find a more cost-effective alternative. I chose the latter and went ahead and purchased a domain name for you (I might have faked your identity hihihi) and I created a first site here. However, I didn't like it one bit. My babe, deserves something eloquent and sophisticated and the template wouldn't allow me to do what I would like.

So, I spent 24-48 hours learning how to code, so that I could build you this site.

I am sorry for the times I looked a mess when you video called.

I combed through your linkedin, academia, research gate profiles and a few photos of yours to use on the site. Do you recognize this photo? You sent it to me last week when you were on your morning walk in Manchester.

Anyway babe, here is your very own WEB PROPERTY and I am happy to have been able to do this for your birthday. An unusual gift, I know; but knowing that I was doing it for you encouraged me to do a good job. Now that you know about it, can you please give me the info I need to populate the website even further? lol

I love you baby and Happy 31st birthday.

Oh, by the way, here is your other gift!

Love, Rho
