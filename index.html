Short Bio

I am a PhD Candidate in Global Governance and Human Security, researching linkages between migration and security policies in South-South migration. I have a keen interest in the developmental prospects of regional integration and the synergies and opportunities which it promises in a globalized world with a rapidly shifting center of gravity. I have a similar interest in questions of international security as regards transnational organized crime, and migration.
